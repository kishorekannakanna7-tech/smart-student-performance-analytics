## 🤖 AI Prediction System

### Prediction Algorithm
Weighted scoring system:
- Attendance: 20%
- Internal Marks: 30%
- Assignment Marks: 20%
- Previous Marks: 30%

### Risk Level Classification
- **High Risk**: Attendance < 60% OR Internal < 40
- **Medium Risk**: Attendance < 75% OR Internal < 50
- **Low Risk**: Above all thresholds

### Grade Prediction
- A+ (90-100): Excellent
- A (80-89): Very Good
- B+ (70-79): Good
- B (60-69): Satisfactory
- C (50-59): Pass
- F (<50): Fail

### Pass Probability
- 95%: Score ≥ 75
- 80%: Score ≥ 60
- 60%: Score ≥ 50
- 30%: Score < 50
